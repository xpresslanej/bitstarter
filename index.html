The goal of Bashbuildr is to make planning an event easy while minimizing the risk for planners and vendors

Bashbuildr combines the best elements of services like Yelp, Evite, OpenTable, and Kickstarter to offer a unique approach that not only streamlines the event planning process, but also offers a way for organizers to raise and collect money first before committing. Bashbuildr also offers assurance to vendors that they will be compensated even for last minute cancellations.

Another event scheduler application, really?

Bashbuildr is different from other event schedulers in that it addresses the funding of an event upfront. Often times, an organizer has a great idea for an event, but is usually the one that needs to not only make all of the reservations but also put up the cash initially. Bashbuildr helps an organizer determine the costs up front and then establishes that as a funding goal with a timeline in which to raise the money before committing to the event.

Attendees can pay into the event fund ahead of time. If the goal is not reached by the deadline, the event organizer has the option to commit the remaining funds themselves, or cancel the event in which case all of the contributors are refunded automatically (minus a small transaction fee).
